# Claude Review Guide

Review evidence before copy.

## Checkpoint A

Verify:

- The full Phase 1 coverage table and its total row.
- The 4-quadrant census and every denominator.
- The early data gate.
- Hook eligibility and source completeness.
- Cross-foot identities.
- Hero screenshot legibility and frozen-number consistency.
- Privacy limitations, classifier limitations, and judgment calls.

Do not approve a performance claim. Inbox history demonstrates competitor
behavior only.

## Checkpoint B

Verify:

- Post, pinned comment, images, public guide, delivery playbook, and internal
  launch task.
- Confirmed public links and logged-out access.
- Full Git-history privacy scan.
- Clean installation from the immutable tag.
- Package validation against the frozen manifest.
- All residual-risk disclosures from `PLAN.md`.

Nothing may be distributed before Zach records clearance.
